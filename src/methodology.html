Methodology

This tool estimates the indirect water consumption associated with digital activities. The water usage does not come from the user directly, but from the infrastructure required to deliver digital services.

Assumptions

The following average values are used for estimation:

Activity Estimated water usage Source
TikTok / Reddit 16.0 L / hour [1]
Other Social Media 6.0 L / hour [1]
HD Streaming 9.0 L / hour [2]
Online Meetings 12.0 L / hour [2]
LLM / AI 0.01 L / query [2]
Limitations
  • Values are averages, not exact measurements
  • Infrastructure efficiency varies by region
Purpose

This calculator is designed for educational and awareness purposes. It helps visualize the hidden environmental impact of digital usage, not to provide precise accounting.
